What is the Enneagram?

The Enneagram is an ancient system that dates as far back as the 4th century. Although its origins may be ancient, the Enneagram started to adapt and modernize in the 1970s. Today’s Enneagram is an in-depth system that calculates the user’s personality type through a series of tests. These types describe and conceptualize how people interact with the world and their emotions. The Enneagram has nine personality types that correlate together on a nine-point model. The Gospel

The Word Gospel in Greek translates to “good news”. The good news is this: We can have a relationship with God The Father through His son Jesus Christ. We live in a broken world with broken people. Jesus meets us in our brokenness and offers abundant Life (John 10:10) to all who are willing to receive him.

Whose am I?

Humans are made in the image of God and we are designed to be in a relationship with him. Because we are born into sin and separation from him, our spirits yearn to reunite with our original Creator. When we come to know Christ and receive the Holy Spirit, we become children of God. The Spirit himself bears witness with our spirit that we are children of God (Romans 8:16) When we come to Christ, we belong to him and when we realize whose we are, we then know who we are.

Who am I?

The Enneagram is a powerful tool that helps us better understand who we are, who others are, and view life in a different way. However, it is only in finding our true identity in Christ that we will be set free and truly see ourselves for who we really are in Him.

How Does The Enneagram Work With The Gospel?

Understanding Ourselves

Once we know ourselves more intimately, we will be able to understand the Gospel in deeper and richer ways.

Our Language

The Enneagram can help us hear the Gospel and its truths in our “mother tongue,” or our personality Type’s language, helping our heart to better receive it.

Clearity in Core Fears

You will see how apart from Christ, each personality Type is running away from its Core Fear, running towards its Core Desire, stumbling over its Core Weakness, and thirsty to hear its Core Longing.

Health Levels

the grace of God, we can ask the Holy Spirit to help us navigate our internal worlds and bring our personality Types back to their healthiest paths.
