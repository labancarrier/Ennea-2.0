What is the Enneagram?

The Enneagram is a system of personality typing that describes patterns in how people conceptualize the world and manage their emotions. The Enneagram model describes nine different personality types and maps each of these types on a nine-pointed diagram which helps to illustrate how the types relate to one another. How The Enneagram Works With the Gospel?

Understanding Ourself

Once we know ourselves more intimately, we will be able to understand the Gospel in deeper and richer ways.

Our Language

The Enneagram can help us hear the Gospel and its truths in our “mother tongue,” or our personality Type’s language, helping our heart to better receive it.

Clearity in Core Fears

You will see how apart from Christ, each personality Type is running away from its Core Fear, running towards its Core Desire, stumbling over its Core Weakness, and thirsty to hear its Core Longing.

Health Levels

the grace of God, we can ask the Holy Spirit to help us navigate our internal worlds and bring our personality Types back to their healthiest paths.

How the Gospel Comforts Each Type.

One of the unique characteristics of the Enneagram typology is its identification of our core fears. Here are Bible verses that speak directly into the core fear of each Enneagram type.

Type 1—The Reformer

2 Corinthians 12:9: “But he said to me, “My grace is sufficient for you, for my power is made perfect in weakness.” Therefore I will boast all the more gladly about my weaknesses, so that Christ’s power may rest on me.”.

Type 2—The Helper

Romans 8:37-39 “No, in all these things we are more than conquerors through him who loved us. For I am convinced that neither death nor life, neither angels nor demons, neither the present nor the future, nor any powers, neither height nor depth, nor anything else in all creation, will be able to separate us from the love of God that is in Christ Jesus our Lord.

Type 3—The Performer

Colossians 3:23 “Whatever you do, work at it with all your heart, as working for the Lord, not for human masters”

The Individualist

Psalm 139:14 “I praise you because I am fearfully and wonderfully made; your works are wonderful, I know that full well.”

Type 5—The Investigator

Psalm 118:6-7 “The Lord is with me; I will not be afraid. What can man do to me? The Lord is with me; he is my helper.”

Type 6—The Loyalist

Type 7—The Enthusiast

Type 8—The Challenger

Isaiah 52: 12: “But you will not leave in haste or go in flight; for the LORD will go before you, the God of Israel will be your rear guard.”

Type 9—The Peacemaker

1 Peter 1:2 “You were chosen according to the purpose of God the Father and were made a holy people by his Spirit, to obey Jesus Christ and be purified by his blood. May grace and peace be yours in full measure.”
