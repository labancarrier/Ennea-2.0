Type 2: The Helper

2- The Helper. This will be the overveiw. We have named personality type One The Reformer because Ones have a “sense of mission” that leads them to want to improve the world in various ways, using whatever degree of influence they have. They strive to overcome adversity—particularly moral adversity—so that the human spirit can shine through and make a difference. They strive after “higher values,” even at the cost of great personal sacrifice.

Type 2 in brief

Ones are conscientious and ethical, with a strong sense of right and wrong. They are teachers, crusaders, and advocates for change: always striving to improve things, but afraid of making a mistake. Well-organized, orderly, and fastidious, they try to maintain high standards, but can slip into being critical and perfectionistic. They typically have problems with resentment and impatience. At their Best: wise, discerning, realistic, and noble. Can be morally heroic.

  • Fear: Of being corrupt/evil, defective
  • Basic Desire: To be good, to have integrity, to be balanced
  • Enneagram One with a Nine-Wing: "The Idealist"
  • Enneagram One with a Two-Wing: "The Advocate"

Type 2 Common Sins

Pride - When expectations and emotional needs are left unmet, pride can begin to creep in. This comes from a Twos inner desire to be worthy of love. When the need to be loved is unacknowledged Twos can slip into putting others’ needs first and can unintentionally (or intentionally) expect something in return. When they believe they are not given or shown the love and respect they deserve, this can lead to the resentment of others.

Encouragement: To acknowledge the desires God has put in your heart. Your desire to be loved and be worthy are already fulfilled in Him and that is a truth you get to live from, not for. When you find yourself beginning to serve and love others out of expectation, take a second to remember you have everything you need and love from the abundant love given to you!
