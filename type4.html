Type 4: The Individualist

4- The Individualist. It was named this type The Individualist because Fours maintain their identity by perceiving themselves as fundamentally different from others. Fours feel that they are different than other human beings, and consequently, that no one can understand them or love them adequately. They often see themselves as uniquely gifted, possessing unique, one-of-a-kind gifts, but also as uniquely disadvantaged or flawed. More than any other type, Fours are acutely aware of and interested in their personal differences and deficiencies.

Type 4 in Found in the Bible

The ancient prophets had a way of both embodying and articulating very off the beaten path messages for the sake of justice and restoration. They were unafraid to dive straight to the uncomfortable aspects of life and became very well acquainted with their own brokenness, as well as that of their people.

This story shows how Jeremiah follows a very peculiar set of instructions to articulate a more complex truth about God to the people he’s responsible for. This pattern happens many times with other prophets; Hosea willingly marries a woman who he knows will be unfaithful, Isaiah wanders naked in the wilderness, and Jeremiah remains unmarried as a symbol.

The prophets exhibit four characteristics by having a high capacity for the peculiar, uncomfortable, and unfamiliar, so capacity for the peculiar, uncomfortable, and unfamiliar, so that the rest of the world can understand deep and complex truths that they currently are not grasping. They invoke imagination, sorrow, pain, and wonder in the lives of others.

Type 4 Common Sins

Ones are conscientious and ethical, with a strong sense of right and wrong. They are teachers, crusaders, and advocates for change: always striving to improve things, but afraid of making a mistake. Well-organized, orderly, and fastidious, they try to maintain high standards, but can slip into being critical and perfectionistic. They typically have problems with resentment and impatience. At their Best: wise, discerning, realistic, and noble. Can be morally heroic.

Type 4 Freed in from the Gospel

Pray and ask the Holy Spirit to enable you to truly know, believe, and trust that Jesus Christ’s life, death, and resurrection perfectly satisfies all of your deepest longings. Rest in knowing that you are seen and understood for your unique and authentic self.

You are loved and cherished for your ability to feel deeply. You are rescued from any and all shame and inadequacy. You are 100% secure in being His beloved and cherished child. He sees your radiance, creativity, and individuality, and wants you to use it to bless the world. You are not your emotions, and you belong as God's beloved child.
